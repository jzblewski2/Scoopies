Scoopie's Custard

Welcome to Scoopie's, where everyone is family.

We have been family owned and operated since 2003. We offer up to 15 flavors at any time, and even offer seasonal flavors. Made with all fresh ingredients, safe from artificial dyes, flavors and sweetners.

Meet the Owners


Peter Custard

Peter has been the owner since Scoopie's first opened in 2003. He has had a love for frozen custard since he was a young boy. He wanted to spread his love by opening a welcoming, family friendly establishment.


Roseanne Scoopie

Roseanne is Peter's daugther and has been an owner since she turned 18. When she was just four years old she told her father her dream was to have her very own frozen custard shoppe. With that Scoopie's Custard was created.
